The Team

Srikar Founder X-Google PM Coder/Data Nerd

Srikar Prasad is the founder of Argot.

After working for 2.5 years as a Product Manager at Google, Srikar quit his job to make coding more accessible to everyone.

Before this, Srikar did his MBA at Yale SOM, was the eng-lead for a healthcare startup, and studied Math at the University of Pennsylvania.

When he's not coding, Srikar loves playing music, historical fiction, tinkering, and loudly singing out of ear-shot.

Stefie Advisor Brown Professor Genius

Stefanie Tellex is Argot's tech advisor.

Stefanie did her PhD at MIT's Media Lab, and is currently an assistant professor of Computer Science at Brown University

Stefanie's research focuses on robot-human interactions. She has done ground-breaking work on robot understanding of human-given instructions.

Stefanie trains machines, students, and cats.

Sean Advisor Coder/Enrepreneur Builder

Sean Shadmand is Argot's startup advisor.

Sean is a serial entrepreneur and startup advisor. He was the founder and CEO of Socialize and AppMakr as well as a half dozen other startups that had a successful exit.

In addition to his own startups, Sean is a thought-leader in entrepreneurship and a mentor to dozens of aspiring entrepreneurs.

Elon Advisor Venture Capitalist Dreamer

Elon Boms is Argot's funding advisor.

An entrepreneur himself, Elon founded LaunchCapital, a VC with offices in New Haven, New York, Boston, and San Francisco.

Elon is a fellow SOM alumn and is deeply involved in the Elm City community. He is also a generous mentor to young entrepreneurs and an avid traveler.

The Dream

What does programming 2.0 look like? What if building a full stack application were as easy as making a powerpoint presentation? What if writing code were not a barrier to innovation or data-driven insights? What kind of a world would we live in?

Argot is trying to find out. We want to build a natural language interpreter to convert written instructions to structured, sensible, code. We are starting with data science, our passion, and the heart of product development.

Let's make something awesome, together.
